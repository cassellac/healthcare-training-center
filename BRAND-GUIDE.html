Brand Foundation

Healthcare Training Center is a people-first training platform for healthcare professionals. Built by a lawyer whose personal story drives the mission: this system is broken, but you can be the change in it.

Core Values

Trustworthy

Credentialed, legally sound, professionally authoritative. We know healthcare law and compliance inside out.

Warm

Patient-centered, human, never condescending. We speak like a trusted mentor who happens to be an expert.

Empowering

Learners leave more capable than when they arrived. We're here to help you succeed and make a difference.

Clear

No jargon when avoidable, no confusion, no bureaucratic language. Direct communication, always.

Core Promise

Healthcare professionals who complete training here treat people better — not because the law says they have to, but because they understand why it matters.

Voice & Tone

Warm authority. Credentialed and legally sound, but never bureaucratic or condescending. The brand sounds like a trusted mentor who happens to be an expert.

Brand Personality

  • Trustworthy: Credentialed, legally sound, professionally authoritative
  • Warm: Patient-centered, human, never condescending
  • Empowering: Learners leave more capable than when they arrived
  • Clear: No jargon, no confusion, no bureaucratic language

Copy Guidelines

  • Person: Speak to learners as "you"; the organization is "we"
  • Casing: Title Case for headers, sentence case for body copy, UPPERCASE for badges and labels
  • Taglines: Love the rule of three — "Enter. Learn. Lead." / "Learn. Grow. Make a Difference."
  • Why framing: Every room carries a "Why This Room Matters" note written from a patient-advocacy lens
  • Emoji: Never used. The brand reads as professional.

Copy Examples

  • "Your Path to Better Care Starts Here" — lobby welcome
  • "Where Healthcare Teams Get It Right" — confidence without arrogance
  • "Train With Purpose. Care With Confidence." — outcome-focused
  • "Hi! We're here to help you succeed." — help desk voice
  • "Ask your admin to unlock." — locked room tooltip

Usage Guidelines

Do's

  • Use navy + teal as your primary color combo
  • Apply room colors purposefully — use them to show room identity, not decoration
  • Keep layouts clean and spacious (generous section breaks)
  • Respect the 8px spacing grid throughout
  • Always include visible focus states for keyboard navigation
  • Test color contrast — meet WCAG 2.1 AA minimum (4.5:1 text, 3:1 UI)
  • Use Phosphor icons exclusively for all UI iconography
  • Keep motion purposeful — respect prefers-reduced-motion

Don'ts

  • Don't use heavy gradients or busy textures — keep the brand clean
  • Don't invent new colors — stick to the palette provided
  • Don't use emoji for iconography — use Phosphor icons instead
  • Don't make marketing copy sound cold or clinical
  • Don't sacrifice accessibility for aesthetics
  • Don't use the room colors interchangeably — each room = one specific color
  • Don't create decorative animations — motion signals state change only
  • Don't use Playfair Display outside of certificates

Spacing & Layout

  • Base unit: 8px grid throughout
  • Section breaks: 48–96px vertical padding between major sections
  • Content width: 1280px maximum, centered
  • Mobile first: Design for 375px mobile up to desktop
  • Card padding: 24px (--space-5) default
  • Border radius: 16px for room/feature cards, 8px for buttons and course cards
