🚀 Getting Started

For a quick overview: Start with the Brand Guide — it's a visual walkthrough of everything.

For marketing materials: Use the Email, Social, and Presentation templates as starting points. Customize the copy and you're done.

For design decisions: Keep the Design Skill open as a reference. It has color codes, spacing rules, and common Q&As.

For uploading to AI tools: Screenshot key sections from the Brand Guide, copy color codes and typography specs into your prompts, reference icon names from the Icon Library.
