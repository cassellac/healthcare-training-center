Social Media Do's & Don'ts

  • Do: Use bright, saturated room colors (they pop on social feeds)
  • Do: Lead with the room name and icon (visual consistency across platforms)
  • Do: Ask questions or encourage engagement ("What's your biggest challenge?")
  • Do: Share quick wins and testimonials from real learners
  • Do: Use the rule of three in captions ("Learn. Grow. Make a Difference.")
  • Don't: Overuse emoji — keep it professional but warm
  • Don't: Make posts too long on Twitter/Instagram (character limits matter)
  • Don't: Use clinical jargon in social copy — stay accessible
  • Don't: Post without proofing — this is brand-facing content
  • Don't: Forget alt text on images (accessibility matters)

Posting Schedule Tips

  • LinkedIn: Tuesday–Thursday, 8am–10am or 5pm–6pm (peak engagement for healthcare professionals)
  • Instagram: Daily Stories, 3–4 Feed posts/week. Best times: 12pm–1pm, 5pm–6pm
  • Twitter/X: 2–3 posts/day. Healthcare industry is active during work hours (7am–5pm)
  • Consistency: Pick a schedule and stick to it. Your audience comes to expect you
